    Get To School 5

Description

In "Get to School 5," players embark on a hilariously perilous journey through the chaotic halls of academia, where every locker hides a potential ambush, every teacher has a secret side hustle, and the cafeteria food is rumored to have a mind of its own. As our dashing protagonist, channeling the suave energy of Aiden, aka Shakable, players must navigate through a minefield of procrastination, dodgeballs, and pop quizzes while charming their way through the social stratosphere. With a dazzling array of hairstyles and outfits inspired by the legendary heartthrob, players can woo classmates, outsmart bullies, and maybe even find the legendary lost homework, all while trying not to trip over their own shoelaces. Get ready to embark on the most epic quest of all: surviving high school!
